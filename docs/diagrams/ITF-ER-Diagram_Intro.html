About This System

This database schema represents a comprehensive tournament management system for ITF (International Tennis Federation) senior tournaments, designed for players aged 60+ and 65+ across male and female categories.

The system handles tournament registration, draw generation, match tracking with detailed scoring (including tie-breaks and super tie-breaks), and sophisticated ranking calculations based on players' best 4 results over rolling 12-month periods.

Key Features

  • 6 NEW Tie-Break Fields: Comprehensive scoring for set tie-breaks (7 points) and super tie-breaks (10 points)
  • Realistic Entry Timestamps: Strategic player behavior with 3-4 last-minute entries per draw
  • Automated Ranking System: Rankings calculated every Monday at 20:00 UTC based on best 4 results
  • Draw Management: Automatic draw generation with seeding rules based on draw size
  • Points System: Stage-based points allocation with first-round loser receiving zero points
  • Historical Data: 54 weeks of tournament history (January 2025 - January 2026)
  • Clean Date Fields: No unnecessary timestamps on date-only fields
